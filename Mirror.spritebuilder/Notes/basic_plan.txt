A puzzle game where the user has to obtain the key to a door in order to move on to the next level. 
Constants in each level:
    - there is a door
    - there is a key
    - the player can jump to a certain height
    - the player can move left and right
    - there is an invisible line down the middle of the level
        - things that happen on onside also happen on the other side, but flipped
    - the player can only exit the level on one side of the door
    - the door will not open without the key
Level 1:
    - key is already in the door
    - player must get to the door
